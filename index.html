Doli — Privacy Policy

Effective date: July 6, 2026 · Last updated: July 9, 2026

Doli (“the App”) respects your personal and health information and complies with applicable data-protection laws. This policy explains, transparently, what information the App handles and how.

1. Core principle — all your data stays on your device

The App does not transmit or collect any of your personal health information to any external server. Everything you enter (fasting records, weight, diet-injection logs, and so on) is stored only on your own device. No one — including the developer — can access it.

  • The App’s core features work without any sign-up.
  • Your health, fasting, and weight data live only on your device, with no cloud backup.
  • Google Sign-In is optional; even if you use it, your email is stored only on your device (never sent to any third party — see Section 3).

Uninstalling the App permanently deletes all data stored on your device; it cannot be recovered.

2. Information the App handles

The following is entered by you or stored on your device to make features work. All of the items below are stored only locally, on your device.

Weight and diet-injection logs may constitute sensitive (health) information under data-protection law. The App protects them by keeping them only on your device and never collecting them on a server. Entering this information is entirely optional; you can use the App’s core features without it.

3. Third-party services and automatically processed information

The App uses the third-party services below. In the course of using them, some information may be processed by each provider.

a. Advertising (Google AdMob)

The App uses Google AdMob to show ads on the free version. To serve and measure ads, AdMob may collect device information such as an advertising identifier (Advertising ID).

  • Google’s data practices: https://policies.google.com/privacy
  • You can reset your advertising ID or limit personalized ads in your device settings.
  • Purchasing Premium (ad removal) stops ads and the related data collection.

b. Google Sign-In (optional)

Only if you choose to sign in, the App uses Google Sign-In and receives your email address. This email is stored only on your device to show your signed-in status and is not sent to the developer’s servers or any third party. Sign-in is optional, and signing out within the App immediately deletes the stored email. The Google Sign-In process itself is governed by Google’s Privacy Policy (https://policies.google.com/privacy).

c. In-app purchases (Google Play Billing)

Premium purchases are processed through Google Play Billing. Payment details (such as card numbers) are never collected or stored by the App; they are handled entirely by Google.

d. Health Connect (optional)

Only if you choose to, the App may request permission to read weight data from Health Connect on your device. This data is used only for on-device processing and is never transmitted externally. You can revoke the permission at any time in your device settings.

5. Retention and deletion

Because the App does not collect information on any server, there is no separate retention or deletion process. You can delete data stored on your device instantly by using the reset feature in the App or uninstalling the App.

6. Children’s privacy

The App is not directed to children under 14 and does not knowingly collect children’s personal information. Fasting is not recommended for growing children or adolescents.

7. Your rights

Because all data resides only on your device, you have full control over your information. You can view, edit, and delete your data within the App at any time.

8. Changes to this policy

This policy may be revised in response to changes in law or App features. We will announce any changes through the App or the store listing.

Appendix — Health disclaimer

Doli is an informational app that supports healthy lifestyle habits. It is not a medical device and does not provide medical diagnosis, treatment, or prescription. The diet-injection (e.g. GLP-1) logging feature only helps you keep your own records; it provides no medical advice on dosage, timing, or anything else. Always consult your doctor or a qualified professional for any medical decision.

돌리(이하 “본 앱”)는 이용자의 개인정보와 건강 정보를 소중히 여기며, 「개인정보 보호법」 및 관련 법령을 준수합니다. 본 방침은 본 앱이 어떤 정보를 어떻게 다루는지 투명하게 안내합니다.

1. 핵심 원칙 — 모든 데이터는 기기 안에만

본 앱은 이용자의 개인 건강 정보를 외부 서버로 전송하거나 수집하지 않습니다. 이용자가 입력하는 모든 정보(단식 기록, 체중, 다이어트 주사 투여 기록 등)는 오직 이용자의 기기 내부에만 저장됩니다. 개발자를 포함한 그 누구도 이 정보에 접근할 수 없습니다.

  • 앱의 핵심 기능은 회원가입 없이 이용할 수 있습니다.
  • 건강·단식·체중 데이터는 클라우드 백업 없이 기기 안에만 있습니다.
  • Google 로그인은 선택 사항이며, 이용 시에도 이메일은 기기 안에만 저장됩니다(제3자에 전송 없음 — 3장 참고).

앱을 삭제하면 기기에 저장된 모든 데이터도 함께 삭제되며, 복구할 수 없습니다.

가. 광고 (Google AdMob)

무료 이용자에게 광고를 제공하기 위해 Google AdMob을 사용합니다. AdMob은 광고 제공 및 측정을 위해 광고 식별자(Advertising ID) 등 기기 정보를 수집할 수 있습니다.

나. Google 로그인 (선택)

이용자가 로그인을 선택하는 경우에 한해 Google 로그인(Google Sign-In)을 사용하며, 이때 이용자의 이메일 주소를 제공받습니다. 이 이메일은 로그인 상태 표시를 위해 기기 내부에만 저장되며, 개발자의 서버나 제3자에게 전송되지 않습니다. 로그인은 선택 사항이고, 앱 내 로그아웃 시 저장된 이메일은 즉시 삭제됩니다. Google 로그인 과정 자체는 Google의 개인정보처리방침(https://policies.google.com/privacy)에 따릅니다.

프리미엄 기능 구매는 Google Play 결제 시스템을 통해 처리됩니다. 결제 정보(카드번호 등)는 본 앱이 수집하거나 저장하지 않으며, 전적으로 Google에 의해 처리됩니다.
